Summary

SOS Alarm operates the Swedish emergency number 112. Their vision is to create a safer Sweden. SOS Alarm is providing the general public with assistance and information in times of need. This is how their life-saving app was created.

I worked as a lead designer doing the design process planning, research, concept design, user and target group interviews/testing, UI design and delivering together with the developers as part of an agile team.
